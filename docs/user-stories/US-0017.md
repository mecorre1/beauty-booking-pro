## Story
As a client, I want to select my service first and then see only the slots that fit my chosen service so that I don't waste time on unavailable options.

## Requirements
- Booking flow starts with service selection (type + gender + location)
- After service selection, available slots are fetched based on service duration
- Slots are grouped by time of day: morning (before 12), afternoon (12-17), evening (after 17)
- Slots shorter than the selected service duration are excluded
- At-home bookings account for travel buffer in availability computation (internal only, not shown to client)
- Empty groups (no slots in morning/afternoon/evening) are hidden

## Acceptance Criteria
- [ ] `GET /api/public/availability?service_id=X&week=YYYY-WW` is called after service selection
- [ ] Results are grouped into morning, afternoon, evening on the frontend
- [ ] Groups with no available slots are not rendered
